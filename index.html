About Us

A Different Kind
of Builder

Kepler Property Group was founded on years of hands-on experience in residential construction, civil works, carpentry, and project management across Melbourne and the Mornington Peninsula. We bring a practical, detail-first approach to every project, combining strong trades knowledge with a genuine focus on design, communication, and client experience.

Our goal is simple: deliver homes and developments that are built to a high standard and designed to stand the test of time.
